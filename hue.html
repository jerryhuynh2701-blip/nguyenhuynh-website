About Hue

Hue is located in central Vietnam and is a historic city in central Vietnam. It was once the capital of the Nguyen Dynasty and is well known for its Imperial Citadel, pagodas, and royal tombs. The Perfume River runs through the city, giving it a peaceful charm.

Hue is also well known for its cuisine, which is often described as the most delicate and refined in Vietnam. Dishes like Bún bò Huế (spicy beef noodle soup)

Famous Landmarks of Hue

The Perfume River flows gently through Hue, creating a peaceful atmosphere. On its banks stands the iconic Thien Mu Pagoda, one of the most famous religious sites in Vietnam.

The best famous foods in Hue that I can't stop craving

  1. Bún bò Huế (spicy beef noodle soup)
  2. Bánh bèo (steamed rice cakes)
  3. Bánh nậm (flat steamed rice dumplings with shrimp)
